# 📈 Inventory & Sales Forecasting Dashboard

A predictive tool for Supply Chain and Inventory managers to anticipate demand and avoid stockouts.

## How it works:
1. **Upload:** Provide a CSV with `Date` and `Sales`.
2. **Analyze:** The app uses **Prophet** to decompose your data into trends (growth) and seasonality (weekly/yearly patterns).
3. **Forecast:** Adjust the slider to see demand for the next 30 to 365 days.
4. **Stock Insight:** Automatically calculates the total inventory units required for the forecast period.
